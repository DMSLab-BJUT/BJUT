Tong Li

Dr. Tong Li holds a lecturer position in the Faculty of Information Technology at the Beijing University of Technology. He received his Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from the University of Trento in 2016, under the supervision of Professor John Mylopoulos. He has been an author or co-author of more than 50 papers in peer-reviewed journals, conferences, or workshops. His research interests are in the areas of security requirements engineering, conceptual modeling, formal method, and data mining. He is a co-editor of ISO/IEC 24392 (Information technology – Security techniques – Security reference model for industrial internet platform).
