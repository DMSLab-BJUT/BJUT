Haiyang Yu

Personal Information

Dr. Haiyang Yu is currently a post-doc of Faculty of Information and Technology, Beijing University of Technology.He has published more than 10 papers in peer-reviewed journals, conferences, or workshops as an author or co-author.

Research Interests

His interests include data integrity auditing, data security, and blockchain.
